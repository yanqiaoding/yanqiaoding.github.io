Personal Information

I obtained my doctor degree in Mathematics at Sun Yat-sen University under the supervision of Professor Jianxun Hu in 2016. In July 2016, I joined the School of Mathematics and Statistics at Zhengzhou University as a Lecturer. From Sept. 2019 to Aug. 2020, I finished one year's postdoc research at IMJ-PRG, Sorbonne University with Professor Ilia Itenberg under the visiting scholar progrem supported by China scholarship council. From Jan. 2024, I am an associate Professor at Zhengzhou University.



Research Interests

Symplectic geometry, algebraic geometry, mirror symmetry
