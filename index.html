Personal Information

I accomplished my Ph.D. degree in Mathematics at Sun Yat-sen University under the supervision of Professor Jianxun Hu in 2016. After that I moved to Zhengzhou University as a Lecturer at the School of Mathematics and Statistics. From Sept. 2019 to Aug. 2020, I did one year's postdoc research at IMJ-PRG, Sorbonne University with Professor Ilia Itenberg under the visiting scholar progrem supported by China scholarship council.

Research Interests

Symplectic topology, enumerative geometry, mathematical physics
